Dojo RecognitionsINTRODUCTION

DOJO recognition is a tool and service to measure DOJO community members in order to grow up forward into Transformational Roles and self development towards personal goals aligned with the Organization professional needs.
This measure and progress utilize Badge recognition system, it means, when ever a member full fit the requirements of the Badge them earn it and continues towards the next Badge Goals, Badge Goals are alignment with the Dojo Goals.
Above all, we seek that each and every one enjoy their development in the DOJO community, through challenges and self-improvement.

Dojo RecognitionsWhite Code Snippet

DESCRIPTION
White Snippet Code is the fundamental stage at Dojo for People who pursuit develop and self improvement to engage IT products/initiatives/projects in the Information Age

EARNING CRITERIA
- 12 Week of Dojo participation
- Fundamental understanding of System Thinking
- Fundamental understanding of Transformational Project
- 3 Transformational practices deeply discussed
- 1 Dojo session as a facilitator
- 10 Dojo Mentor & Mentee Sessions

Dojo RecognitionsBLACK Code Snippet

DESCRIPTION
Black Snippet Code is the advanced stage at Dojo for People who pursuit develop and self improvement to engage IT products/initiatives/projects in the Information Age

EARNING CRITERIA
- Requires White Code Snippet Badge
- Fundamental understanding of Lean Persona
- Fundamental understanding of Lean Product
- 6 Transformational practices deeply discussed
- 2 Dojo session as a facilitator
- 1 Developed practice
- 80 Hours in a Transformational project
